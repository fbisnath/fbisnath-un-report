This report was prepared for the UN. It analyses the relationship between a countrys GDP, life expectancy, and CO2 emissions. Our goal was to determine to what degree a countrys economic strength or weakness may be related to its public health status and impact on climate pollution. We hypothesise that both life expectancy and CO2 emissions will increase with a countrys GDP.

The above plot shows the relationship between the GDP per capita and life expectancy for a total of 142 countries. Economic wealth ranged from a minimum of $ 312 to a maximum of 4.1283^{4} per capita.
